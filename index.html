Rooted in Rosario

A place with history.A hub for today.

For generations, The Beach House has been where Rosario comes together. Today, Blackfish Café carries that legacy forward — bringing people together over great food, good coffee, and unforgettable views.

From early-morning espresso to lunch on the lawn and private celebrations by the water, it's a casual, elevated gathering place built around the easy rhythm of island life.
