Getting HereDrive, sail, hike, or fly in.

Tucked into Rosario Harbor on the east side of Orcas Island, Blackfish Café at The Beach House is easy to reach by nearly every form of Pacific Northwest transport.

Address 1624 Rosario Road, Eastsound, WA View on Google Maps

By Car

Free parking is available for guests. Need a ride? Three Orcas Island taxi services serve the harbor:

  • Scenic Shuttle (360) 298-8414
  • Keith's Taxi (360) 298-1639
  • Orcas Island Taxi (360) 376-8294

By Boat

Day-use moorage at Rosario Harbor is available for guests. Purchase moorage online at rosarioharbor.com and bring your receipt.

By Foot

Camping at Moran State Park? The café is an easy walk from Cascade Lake — about 1.25 miles from the North End campground (1.5 mi from South End, 2 mi from Midway).

Hike around the lake, follow the spillway down to the harbor — roughly 360 ft of elevation change.

By Seaplane

Rosario Harbor is a scheduled stop for Kenmore Air and Friday Harbor Seaplanes.

Flying your own floatplane? Call the Marina at (360) 472-4866.

Rooted in Rosario

A place with history.A hub for today.

For generations, The Beach House has been where Rosario comes together. Today, Blackfish Café carries that legacy forward — bringing people together over great food, good coffee, and unforgettable views.

From early-morning espresso to lunch on the lawn and private celebrations by the water, it's a casual, elevated gathering place built around the easy rhythm of island life.
